## About

Homework Journal is a simple, website I made for my junior cycle Classroom-Based Assessment (CBA).  
It allows users to add, organize, and save homework assignments directly in their browser.

Made by Aarondoran and Maks Kasprzak.

---

## Features

- **Add new homwework** add details such as subject, due date, and extra notes.
- **Categorize assignments** by subject for quick access.
- **Save homework entries** to your device as a JSON file (acts like your personal homework database & Provides Extra security).
- **Load your homework journal** from a JSON file at any time.
- **Works for everyone**—no logins or accounts needed, all data is managed on your device.
- **User-friendly interface** suitable for junior & senior cycle students.

## How to Use

1. **Open the Website**  
   Go to the [Homework Journal Website](https://aarondoran.github.io/Homework-journal/).

2. **Add Assignments**  
   Type in your homework details (subject, due date, notes) as needed.

3. **Save Your Work**  
   Click "Save" to download your homework journal as a JSON file to your device.

4. **Load Your Work**  
   Click "Load" and select your saved JSON file to restore your homework journal.
